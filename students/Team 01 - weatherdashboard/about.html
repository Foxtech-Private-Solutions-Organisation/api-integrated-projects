About Our Weather App

Our Weather App provides accurate, easy-to-understand, and real-time weather updates to help you stay prepared for the day.
This dashboard is powered by reliable weather APIs and can be customised for multiple locations.
Key Features:
1.Real-time current weather updates.
2.Hourly and 7-day forecast.
3.Location-based search & unit conversion.
4.Severe weather alerts for safety.
