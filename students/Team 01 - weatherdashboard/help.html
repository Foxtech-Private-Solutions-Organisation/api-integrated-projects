Help & Support

How do I search for weather in a city?

Use the search box on the Dashboard page to enter a city name. Click "Search" to get current weather and forecast.

How do I add a city to Favorites?

After searching a city, click the "Add to Favorites" button on the Dashboard to save it for quick access later.

How can I view my search history?

Go to the History page to see a list of your recent searched cities and navigate back to their weather details.

What data sources are used?

We use trusted APIs like OpenWeatherMap to provide accurate weather updates.

How do I contact support?

Use the Contact button on the About page to email our support team.
